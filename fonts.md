## Ioskeley Mono
https://github.com/ahatem/IoskeleyMono#ioskeley-mono

A free, open-source alternative to [Berkeley Mono](https://usgraphics.com/products/berkeley-mono) — built by configuring [Iosevka](https://github.com/be5invis/Iosevka) to match its look and feel as closely as possible.
The name is a mashup: Iosevka + Berkeley = Ioskeley.
